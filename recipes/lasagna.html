Lasagna

This is the world's BEST lasagna.
It takes a little work, but it is worth it!

Ingredients (about 8 servings)

For the meat sauce:
  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound froung beef chuck
  • 1/2 medium onion
  • 1/2 large bell pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 3 ounces tomato paste
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch garlic powder and/or garlic salt
  • 1 tablespoon red or white wince vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • Salt
To assemble the lasagna
  • 1/2 pound dry lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 1/2 pounds mozzarella cheese, grated or sliced
  • 1/4 pound freshly grated Parmesan cheese

How to prepare the Lasagna

  1. Start by making the sauce with ground beef, bell peppers, onions, and
    a combo of tomato sauce, tomato paste, and crushed tomatoes. The
    three kinds of tomatoes gives the sauce great depth of flavor.
     
  2. Let this simmer while you boil the noodles and get the cheeses
    ready.    We're using ricotta, shredded mozzarella, and parmesan -- like
    the mix of tomatoes, this 3-cheese blend gives the lasagna great flavor!
     
  3. From there, it's just an assembly job. A cup of meat sauce, a layer of
    noodles, more sauce, followed by a layer of cheese. Repeat until you
    have three layers and have used up all the ingredients.
     
  4. Bake until bubbly and you're ready to eat!
